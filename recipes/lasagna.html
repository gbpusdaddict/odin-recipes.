description

Lasagna is a delicious Italian dish made with layers of pasta, meat, cheese, and sauce.

It is baked in the oven and is one of the most popular comfort foods in the world.

Ingredients

  • lasagna noodles
  • Ground meat
  • Tomato sauce
    • Onions

steps to make lasagna

    cook the noodles
  1. Boil water,add salt
  2. Cook noodles until soft
  3. Drain and set aside

Make the meat sauce

  1. Fry Onions
  2. Add ground meat and cook until brown
  3. Pour in tomato sauce
  4. Add peper salt and herbs
  5. Let it simmer for about 10-15m
