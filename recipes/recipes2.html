Rice

description

Rice is a very common food in most Asian, European, and American countries, and in some parts of Africa. It is commonly served with stew or sauce. It is in seed form — some are white and some are brown.

Ingredients

  • Tomatoes
  • Onions
  • oil commonlly known as groundnut oil
  • Meat
  • Herbs

how to prepare it step my step

  1. boil the rice for like 10 to 15 minutes
  2. Wash and cook until soft or until the water dries

stew

Stew is basically a sauce made from tomatoes, onions, vegetables, meat, and herbs.

Steps

  1. Put a little amount of groundnut oil in a pot
  2. Add Onions and Tomatoes Meat and other sauce and fry for 10 minute
  3. Add a little bit of water
  4. Add seasoning cube and salt cook for like 15m then done
